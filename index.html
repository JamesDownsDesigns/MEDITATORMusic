Music that
makes you think

MEDITATOR started as a concept. James Downs (vocals) had an idea during summer 2017 after his old band broke up. He thought about creating a new studio project, using riffs and basic song ideas he already had in mind. “I approached a friend with a bunch of songs that had a special significance to me”, says James, “and that’s where the name MEDITATOR comes from: to meditate about themes, feelings, and events happening to us as humankind”.

The results of those first ideas was A Darkness Unknown, a concept album produced between 2017 and 2018, and the positive reaction of the album was the catalyst to move from a studio project to a live band. So, James started looking for new members and that’s when Nick Elder (guitar) enters the picture...
