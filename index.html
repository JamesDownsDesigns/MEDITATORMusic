Music that
makes you think

MEDITATOR is a progressive metalcore band based in Nashville, TN, consisting of high-energy aggressive riffs, engaging melodies, and thought provoking lyrics. Its current members are vocalist James Downs, drummer Joshua Ray, bassist Gregory Alexander, and guitarists Nick Elder and Steven Bailey.

Formed in 2017, their first release was an EP titled “A Darkness Unknown” (2018). They debuted their first full-length album titled “World Watcher” in 2021, off of which three singles were released: “Star Gazer”, “Fallen”, and “Dust”
